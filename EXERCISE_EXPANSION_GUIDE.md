This document outlines the expansion of CallistheniX's exercise library from the current 18 exercises to a comprehensive **200+ exercise database** with beginner-specific progressions, difficulty scaling, and wearable integration planning. The expansion is organized by muscle group and includes detailed progression pathways for users at all fitness levels.

### Existing Exercise Structure

Each exercise includes:
- **ID**: Unique identifier (snake_case)
- **Name**: Display name
- **Muscle Groups**: Primary and secondary targets
- **Difficulty**: beginner, intermediate, advanced
- **GIF URL**: Visual demonstration

## Part 3: Beginner-Specific Progression System

### Progressive Difficulty Scaling

The expanded exercise library includes a **6-level progression system** for beginners to safely advance through exercises:

#### Level 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-2)
- **Purpose**: Build basic movement patterns and neuromuscular control
- **Focus**: Form, breathing, body awareness
- **Example Progression**: Wall Push-Up → Incline Push-Up → Knee Push-Up
- **Volume**: 2-3 sets × 8-12 reps
- **Rest**: 60-90 seconds

#### Level 2: Building (Weeks 3-4)
- **Purpose**: Increase volume and endurance
- **Focus**: Consistency, work capacity
- **Example Progression**: Knee Push-Up → Standard Push-Up (assisted)
- **Volume**: 3 sets × 10-15 reps
- **Rest**: 45-60 seconds

#### Level 3: Strengthening (Weeks 5-6)
- **Purpose**: Build strength and muscle
- **Focus**: Progressive overload, form maintenance
- **Example Progression**: Standard Push-Up → Push-Up Variations
- **Volume**: 3-4 sets × 12-15 reps
- **Rest**: 45-60 seconds

#### Level 4: Intermediate (Weeks 7-8)
- **Purpose**: Introduce intermediate exercises
- **Focus**: Advanced movement patterns
- **Example Progression**: Push-Up → Diamond Push-Up / Decline Push-Up
- **Volume**: 3-4 sets × 8-12 reps
- **Rest**: 60-90 seconds

#### Level 5: Advanced Prep (Weeks 9-10)
- **Purpose**: Prepare for advanced movements
- **Focus**: Strength, control, stability
- **Example Progression**: Diamond Push-Up → Archer Push-Up (Assisted)
- **Volume**: 3-4 sets × 5-10 reps
- **Rest**: 90-120 seconds

#### Level 6: Advanced (Weeks 11+)
- **Purpose**: Master advanced calisthenics
- **Focus**: Skill, strength, power
- **Example Progression**: Archer Push-Up → One-Arm Push-Up (Assisted)
- **Volume**: 3-5 sets × 3-8 reps
- **Rest**: 120-180 seconds
